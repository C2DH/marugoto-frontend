Partners

The development of Lives in Transit has been supported by Swiss National Science Foundation (SNF) as part of the Lives in Transit: Steamship Passages in the Late-19th and Early-20th Century World project. This is a collaboration between Prof. Martin Dusinberre (University of Zurich) and Prof. Roland Wenzlhuemer (LMU Munich), jointly funded by the SNF and the German Research Foundation (DFG), 2017-2020. Additional financial support has been provided by the UZH Department of History and the UZH Digital Society Initiative.
