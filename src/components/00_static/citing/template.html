Citation suggestions

To cite the Lives in Transit game:


Martin Dusinberre, Helena Jaskov, Pim Witlox, Daniel McDonald, Leyla Feiner and Henrik Jochum (2020): "Lives in Transit" E-Learning Game, https://www.livesintransit.org


The current topics (June 2020) were authored as follows:

  • Martin Dusinberre et al, “Plantation Lives”
  • Martin Dusinberre et al, “Defining Work”
  • Henrik Jochum et al, “Feeling the Past”
  • Henrik Jochum, Leyla Feiner and Martin Dusinberre, “Tutorial”

For citing of a specific topic, we suggest:


[ topic author et al ], [ topic title ], in Martin Dusinberre, Helena Jaskov, Daniel McDonald, Pim Witlox, Leyla Feiner and Henrik Jochum (2020): "Lives in Transit" E-Learning Game, https://www.livesintransit.org


For example:


Dusinberre, Martin et al, "Defining Work", in Martin Dusinberre, Helena Jaskov, Daniel McDonald, Pim Witlox, Leyla Feiner and Henrik Jochum (2020): "Lives in Transit" E-Learning Game, https://www.livesintransit.org


Or, if you prefer .bib format:

@misc{lit:website, author = "{Martin Dusinberre and Helena Jaskov and Daniel McDonald and Pim Witlox and Leyla Feiner and Henrik Jochum}", title = "Lives in Transit", year = "2020", url = "https://livesintransit.org", note = "[Online; accessed dd.mm.yy]" }
