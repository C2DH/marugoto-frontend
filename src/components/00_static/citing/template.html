Citation suggestions

To cite the Lives in Transit game:


Martin Dusinberre, Helena Jaskov, Pim Witlox, Daniel McDonald and Leyla Feiner (2020): "Lives in Transit" E-Learning Game, https://www.livesintransit.org


For citing of a specific topic, we suggest:


[ topic author et al ], [ topic title ], in Martin Dusinberre, Helena Jaskov, Pim Witlox, Daniel McDonald and Leyla Feiner (2020): "Lives in Transit" E-Learning Game, https://www.livesintransit.org


For example:


Dusinberre, Martin et al, "Defining Work", in Martin Dusinberre, Helena Jaskov, Pim Witlox, Daniel McDonald and Leyla Feiner (2020): "Lives in Transit" E-Learning Game, https://www.livesintransit.org


Or, if you prefer .bib format:

@misc{lit:website, author = "{Martin Dusinberre and Helena Jaskov and Pim Witlox and Daniel McDonald and Leyla Feiner}", title = "Lives in Transit", year = "2020", url = "https://livesintransit.org", note = "[Online; accessed dd.mm.yy]" }
