Citation suggestions

To cite the Lives in Transit game:

Dusinberre, Martin, and the UZH LiT Team (2020): "Lives in Transit" E-Learning Game, https://www.livesintransit.org

For citing of a specific topic, we suggest:

[ topic author et al], [ topic title], in Dusinberre, Martin, and the UZH LiT Team (2020): "Lives in Transit" E-Learning Game, https://www.livesintransit.org

For example:

Dusinberre, Martin et al, "Defining Work", in Dusinberre, Martin, and the UZH LiT Team (2020): "Lives in Transit" E-Learning Game, https://www.livesintransit.org
